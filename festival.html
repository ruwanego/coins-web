COINS Competition consists of three main categories
  • Fiction
  • Non-Fiction
  • Animation
There will be three selection rounds for the COINS competition. 40 Official selections and 16 awards from the final round as competition in various film categories by an international jury.
Award winners will be chosen for the following sections in the competition
  • Best Picture (Gold Coin Award)
  • Fiction
    • Best Directing
    • Best Cinematography
    • Best Editing
    • Best Sound Designing
  • Non-fiction
    • Best Directing
    • Best Cinematography
    • Best Editing
    • Best Sound Designing
  • Animation
    • Best CG Acting
    • xBest Character Design
    • Best Look Development
    • Best VFX
  • Jury Awards
  • Audience Award

Rules and Regulations

  1. All films must be student projects produced as an assignment in a University / Institute / School.
  2. Films must be produced after 01st March 2014.
  3. Animation films should be no less than 1 minute.
  4. Live action short films should be no less than 3 minute and no longer than 25 minutes.
  5. A passport size photograph should be submitted of all student filmmakers
  6. Three still photographs of the film should be submitted (each size of no more than 5MB)
  7. A short synopsis of the film needs to be submitted
  8. Applicants applying online should include contact details of the Dean / Director / Principal of the University / Institute / School respectively in order to verify the student nature of the product.
  9. Online applicants should also submit an application form (Scan of the hard copy) with the official stamp from their respective University / Institute / School heads.
  10. Online applicants should use the official e-mail address of the University / Institute / School when emailing the verification.
  11. The application form can be submitted online or downloaded and submitted as a hard copy
